Welcome to PlanetArkMC – Where imagination meets infrastructure!

Tired of servers that promise "modded RP" but deliver nothing more than cows in suits? Well, not here. PlanetArkMC is the ultimate modded and roleplay experience, where every city, station, and train has a purpose – and usually a horn.

We're constantly evolving, both technically and visually – think of us like Minecraft's version of a growing metro network. We're always on the lookout for creative builders and clever engineers who aren’t afraid to wield a pickaxe and some redstone logic.

Love trains? Good. We build them, ride them, automate them, and sometimes accidentally crash them. Into real-life architecture? Even better. Our world is perfect for recreating your favorite city halls, cathedrals, train stations, or even that weird mall near your grandma's house.

Not into designing trains? No worries – we’ve got that covered. You can focus on operating, maintaining, or constructing the vast infrastructure that makes our universe tick.

Join PlanetArkMC – it's like urban planning, but fun.

Forge, Create, Build, Repeat

We run on Forge, the modding backbone of Minecraft — think of it as the operating system for chaos. At the heart of PlanetArkMC lies Create, the god-tier mod for gears, belts, flywheels, and machines that do things you didn’t know you needed. 

Add Create Deco and you’ve got style: factory walls, cool lamps, and enough bricks to make an architect cry. Create Crafts & Additions brings electricity into the mix, because cranking gears manually is so last season. Then there's Create: Interiors, for players who care about what’s inside the base — tables, chairs, and the kind of aesthetic only true engineers understand. 

And of course, WorldEdit, the holy wand of builders: copy, paste, boom — entire cities in seconds, and no one even saw you sweat.
